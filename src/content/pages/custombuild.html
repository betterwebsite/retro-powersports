Custom Builds

If you want a bike that stands out and rides exactly how you want, our custom builds are the way to go. Whether you're looking for a fully tricked-out race machine, a personalized trail slayer, or just something unique, we build it to fit your style and performance needs. From suspension tuning to engine mods, custom graphics to upgraded components, we make sure every detail is dialed in for the perfect ride.

Every custom build starts with a conversation—we listen to what you want and bring it to life with expert craftsmanship and high-quality parts. No cookie-cutter setups here—just a bike built for you, by riders who know what it takes to create something special. Whether it’s a fresh frame-up build or a complete overhaul of your current setup, we make sure your ride is one of a kind.
